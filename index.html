About me

I am an enthusiastic and tenacious project manager working for local Govenment. I have over 31 years of Housing related experienced gained from a variety of housing organisations, Local authorities, Registered Social Landlords (RSLs) and Tenant Management Organisations. I obtained a B.A Qualification in Professional Housing Studies and Development in the year 2000. In my person life I am a Entreprenuer, and a Qualified Counsellor. I love making things happened hence my journey to becoming a brilliant Web developer. I'm hard working and would consider myself to be a pragmatic and enjoy problem solving. I'm an excellent team player with a great personality.
