About me

Hi, I am an enthusiastic and tenacious project manager working for local Govenment. I have over 31 years of Housing related experience gained from a variety of housing organisations, Local authorities, Registered Social Landlords (RSLs) and Tenant Management Organisations. I have a Batchelors degree in professional Housing Studies and Development, and a Qualified Counsellor. My adventures have no limits. In my person life I am a Entreprenuer, lecturer and trainer. I develope courses that I teach online. I have a passion for learning and welcome my current journey as a Web Developer. My journey as a Web developer will add value and assist me to consolidate my time and projects.

I love making things happened hence my journey to becoming a brilliant Web developer. I'm hard working and would consider myself to be a pragmatic and enjoy problem solving. I'm an excellent team player with a great personality.
